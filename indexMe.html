Scam Message Scanner

This is a scam message scanner that can help you identify potentially harmful messages.

The scanner works by identifying high-risk words and other risk factors. If the scanner identifies a high-risk message, it will warn you to be careful.

The list of high-risk words is stored in a Google Sheets spreadsheet. You can see the list of words by clicking on the following link:

List of High-Risk Words

How to Use the Scanner

To use the scanner, simply enter the text of the message you want to scan in the text box below. Then, click on the "Scan" button.
